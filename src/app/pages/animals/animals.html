Young Stock with Potential

Throughout the year, we receive calves that need homes. We raise them with the same care and attention as our dairy cows, ensuring they grow into healthy, well-adjusted animals.

Heifers are raised as potential dairy or breeding stock, while steers are prepared for sale either privately or at the stockyard for butcher.

Goats

For Sale

Versatile & Friendly

We raise quality goats including wethers (neutered males) and does (females). Goats are intelligent, friendly animals that make great additions to farms or homesteads.

Our goats are well-socialized and handled regularly. They're perfect for brush clearing, companionship, or starting your own herd.
