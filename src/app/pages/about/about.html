Our Story

Welcome to StJo Farm, a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to raising quality livestock with care, respect, and sustainable practices. Our journey began with a passion for agriculture and a commitment to providing the best for our animals and our community.

On our 5.8-acre property, we've created a home for dairy cows, thoroughbred horses, goats, and chickens. Each animal receives individual attention and care, ensuring their health, happiness, and well-being.

Our show dairy cows are the heart of our farm - they're permanent residents who provide fresh, quality milk for our family's personal consumption and to feed our calves and kids. We believe in building community through honest, sustainable farming.
